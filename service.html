Our service

Free Intraday Tips

Intraday tips are short-term stock market recommendations provided by experts to traders to buy and sell stocks within the same trading day based on technical analysis, market trends, and other data.

Share Market Classes

Share market classes refer to courses or training programs that aim to educate individuals on various aspects of the stock market, such as investment strategies, trading techniques, and market analysis.

Open Demat Acc

A demat account is a digital or electronic account that allows investors to hold and trade securities in a paperless form, without the need for physical share certificates. It facilitates easy and secure transactions.

Vehicle Insurance

Vehicle insurance is a contract between the vehicle owner and the insurance company to provide financial protection against loss or damage to the vehicle due to accidents, theft, or natural calamities, in exchange for a premium payment.

Life Insurance

Life insurance is a contract between the policyholder and the insurance company to provide financial protection to the policyholder's family or beneficiaries in case of their untimely death, in exchange for regular premium payments.

Health Insurance

Health insurance is a type of insurance that covers the cost of medical and surgical expenses incurred by the insured individual. It provides financial protection against unexpected healthcare expenses in exchange for regular premium payments.

Business Loan

A business loan is a financial product that provides funds to entrepreneurs or businesses to cover various expenses such as expansion, inventory, equipment, or working capital.

Personal Loan

A personal loan is an unsecured loan that is issued to an individual by a bank or financial institution to meet their personal expenses such as medical bills, home renovations, debt consolidation, or any other financial requirements.

commercial Loan

A commercial loan is a type of loan that businesses can take out to finance commercial activities such as expansion, equipment purchase, or inventory management.

Commodity Market

market where raw materials or primary agricultural products such as gold, silver, crude oil, or grains are traded. It enables buyers and sellers to engage in trading based on future price expectations.

Currency Market

The currency market, also known as the foreign exchange market, is a global decentralized market for the trading of currencies. It facilitates international trade and investment by enabling currency conversion and hedging.

Fix deposits

Fixed deposit (FD) is an investment product offered by banks where customers deposit a lump sum for a fixed term at a fixed interest rate, with a guarantee of principal repayment.
